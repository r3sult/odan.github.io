The question is, what are the favorite verbs in commit messages?<br>
Should I write "Add ..." or "Added ..." ? <br>
Should I write "Remove ..." or "Removed ...." ? <br>

## Conclusion

**Write / good**

* "Added ..."
* "Updated ..."
* "Removed ..."
* "Fixed ..."

Don't write / bad

* "Add ..."
* "Update ..."
* "Remove ..."
* "Delete ..."
* "Fix ..."
